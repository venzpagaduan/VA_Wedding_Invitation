Our Love Story

It all began in a place dedicated to care and healing—a hospital where two lives quietly crossed paths.


October 18, 2022, Venz arrived for his final job interview, a moment filled with anticipation and hope. As he waited, Angeline happened to notice him—just a simple glance, nothing extraordinary at the time, yet it would become the beginning of something meaningful.


Soon after, Venz joined the team as an IT professional, while Angeline continued her work as an accountant. What started as casual conversations in the workplace gradually grew into a genuine friendship. Day by day, they found comfort in each other’s presence—sharing stories, laughter, and small moments that made ordinary days feel a little more special. Over time, that friendship blossomed into something deeper.


After five months of getting to know each other, Venz and Angeline took a step forward and became a couple—choosing not just to share time, but to share their lives.From that unexpected meeting in a hospital hallway to the journey they now continue together, their story is a reminder that love often finds us in the most ordinary places, at the most unexpected times.


And now, they look forward to the next chapter—together.
